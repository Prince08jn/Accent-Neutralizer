Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Accent Neutralizer

How accurate is the accent neutralization?
Our AI achieves 97%+ accuracy in accent neutralization across 25+ languages. The system continuously learns and improves with usage.
Can I use it for live meetings?
Yes! Accent Neutralizer works in real-time for live meetings, video calls, and presentations with minimal latency.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, and you can choose to process data locally. We never store or share your conversations without permission.
What languages are supported?
We support 25+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Hindi, Arabic, and many more.
Can I integrate it with my existing tools?
Yes! We offer integrations with Zoom, Teams, Slack, Google Calendar, and many other popular business tools.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to Professional features. No credit card required to start.
How does team collaboration work?
Teams can join shared transcription rooms, add comments, edit transcripts collaboratively, and share insights in real-time.
Can I export transcripts?
Yes, you can export transcripts in multiple formats including PDF, DOCX, TXT, and JSON. You can also share them via email or direct links.
